About Me

Tattoo Artist

How can you trust a person as long as you don't know that one at least a bit? I want you to know who am I so you can understand WHERE you come. Since I was a child I was in love with the artistic part, with expressing myself using art and I am sure that everyone has something that he/she wishes to express in a way or other. We all know that our body is our temple, as well we know that every temple has its style. This is why i started tattooing, and I've just felt that my body has to express itself at the same time with my mind, and since the first touch with my tools I felt a strong bond between us. That bond, the passion, the wish to express feelings led me on this path, and I want to HELP others to release their PAIN, to be FREE and to be HAPPY
